NOLI ME TANGERE CHARACTERS
Basilio
  • A young boy living in San Diego, and an older brother of Crispin.

  • Escaped punishment by the sacristan mayor, but got shot by the guardia civil and came home seriously injured.
Crispin
  • A young boy living in San Diego, a younger brother of Basilio.

  • Recieved a penalty from Padre Salvi and sacristan mayor after stealing 2 oz, or 32 pesos.
Capitan Tiago
  • An influential businessman in San Diego, father of Maria Clara.

  • Struggled to obey the wills and orders of the friars.

  • Son of a sugar miller.
Padre Damaso
  • Biological father of Maria Clara.

  • Abusive and arrogant friar, and uses corporal punishment on the mistake makers, unlike Padre Salvi who imposes fines instead.
